About Us

Pro_Muster Cleaning Solutions is a woman-operated business servicing the Pretoria East area, Brooklyn areas, and Moralater Park. Assya Ferreira is the owner of Pro_Muster Cleaning Solutions. She takes great pride in providing professional, quality cleaning services and conducting business with the integrity and character you'd expect from a local cleaning company. When we clean, we do it with love, so let us do the cleaning so you can spend more time with your loved ones.

Not only does Pro_Muster Cleaning Services offer quality customer service and house cleaning services, but we also give back to our community.
